Electric Castle is a multi-day music and arts festival held annually at Banffy Castle in Bonțida, Transylvania, Romania — about 30 km from Cluj-Napoca. The festival runs 24/7 across multiple stages, featuring international and local artists, visual arts, comedy, and adventure activities. This page is aimed at international visitors traveling to Electric Castle from abroad or from other Romanian cities.

## HOW TO GET HERE

Electric Castle festival ground is located 30 km (18 miles) outside Cluj-Napoca, in the village of Bonțida, Romania. Cluj-Napoca is well connected both inside Romania and with major European cities through many low-cost flights.

By Plane: Land at Cluj-Napoca International Airport (airportcluj.ro), just 30 km away from the Electric Castle festival site. From the airport, hop on an EC shuttle, a train, a taxi, or an Uber straight to the festival.

By Bus: Non-stop EC shuttle buses run from Cluj-Napoca to the Electric Castle festival site and back all day and night.

By EC Train: The fastest way to get to Electric Castle. Hop on the dedicated EC Train from Cluj-Napoca's Gara Mică station to Jucu, where an EC bus takes you directly to the festival site. The whole journey takes about one hour.

By CFR Trains: Regular CFR trains (Romanian national rail) run to Bonțida, with just a short walk from the train station to the Electric Castle entrance.

By Car: We encourage carpooling to Electric Castle — full cars get free parking and can win cool prizes. Standard parking is available with a pre-purchased pass. Uber and taxis have a fixed-rate drop-off point near the festival.

## WHERE DO YOU SLEEP

Electric Castle offers multiple accommodation options for festival-goers, both on-site and in nearby Cluj-Napoca.

Whether you're all about festival camping life or you prefer a little more comfort, EC Village has options. If camping isn't your thing, Cluj-Napoca has plenty of hotel and apartment accommodation options — just book early since they fill up fast during the festival dates.

In the EC Village (on-site campsite): Our camping has all the perks for a comfy 5-day stay — hot showers, clean restrooms, phone charging stations, 24/7 security, coffee shops, food vendors, a dedicated chill zone, sports areas, and its own stage.

Regular Camping at EC Village: Classic festival vibes — bring your tent, make new friends, and soak in the EC experience 24/7.

Glamping at EC Village: Extra comfort, less hassle. Pre-set tents or wooden cabins with cozy beds, electricity, and more space.

RV Camping at EC Village: Bring your own home on wheels, park, plug in, and let the festival magic take over.

## EXTENDING YOUR STAY — CLUJ-NAPOCA GUIDE

For international visitors arriving before Electric Castle or staying after, Cluj-Napoca is the heart of Transylvania and the region's unofficial capital — a student city filled with art, culture and charm. Arrive a few days before Electric Castle or stay for another week afterwards to soak up the bohemian feeling.

## Walk around the city

Top sights in Cluj-Napoca worth visiting before or after Electric Castle:

Botanical Garden — a 130-year-old green oasis in the centre of the city.
Avram Iancu Square — with the Assumption of the Virgin Orthodox Metropolitan Cathedral, the National Theater and Opera House, and the Statue of Avram Iancu.
Cetatuia — a panoramic view over the city.
Museum Square — with the Franciscan Church and Monastery, Mikes Palace, the Transylvanian History Museum and the Carolina Obelisk.
The walls of the Middle Ages — the second fortified enclosure from the 15th century and the Tower of the Tailors.
The Matthias Corvinus House — where the King of Hungary was born.
The Union Square — with St. Michael's Catholic Church, The Matthias Corvinus Statuary, Aristocratic Palaces (Banffy, Rhedey, Josika, Wass, Filstich-Kemeny) and Shot Pillars.
Wolf's Lane — the Reformed-Calvin Church and the Saint George Statue on one of the most beautiful streets in the city.

Guided tours of Cluj-Napoca: Daily free tours available from 11 AM and 6 PM, starting from The Union Square, and special tours available upon request.

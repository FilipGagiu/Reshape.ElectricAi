HOW TO GET HERE? EASY!
The festival ground is located 30 km (18 miles) outside Cluj-Napoca, in the village of Bonțida.
There are plenty of ways to get there, so take your time and find the option that suits you best.
Cluj-Napoca is super well connected both inside the country as well as with major European cities through many low-cost flights. Once you're there, you can hitch a ride, rent a car if feeling fancy, hop on a bus, or even choo-choo your way there on a train.
By Plane
Land at Cluj-Napoca International Airport
, (airportcluj.ro), just 30 km away. From there, hop on an EC shuttle, a train, a taxi, or an Uber straight to the festival.
By Bus
Non-stop EC shuttle buses run from Cluj-Napoca
to the festival and back. No need to stress - just hop on, ride, and repeat!
By EC Train
This is the fastest way to get to the castle!
Hop on our dedicated EC Train from Cluj-Napoca's Gara Mică station, taking you straight to Jucu, where an EC bus will take you directly to the festival site. The whole journey takes about one hour.
By CFR Trains
CFR trains run regularly to Bonțida,
with just a short walk from the train station to the entrance.
By Car
Driving? We encourage carpooling! Full cars get free parking and can win cool prizes. Standard parking is available with a pre-purchased pass. Ride-sharing? Uber and taxis have a fixed-rate drop-off point.
WHERE DO YOU SLEEP?
Whether you’re all about that festival camping life or you prefer a little more comfort, we’ve got options.
If camping isn’t your thing, Cluj-Napoca has plenty of accommodation options. Just book early - it fills up fast!
In the EC Village
Our camping has all the perks for a comfy 5-day stay: hot showers, clean restrooms, phone charging stations, 24/7 security, coffee shops, food vendors, a dedicated chill zone, sports areas, and even its own stage.
Regular Camping
Classic festival vibes - bring your tent, make new friends, and soak in the EC experience 24/7.
Glamping
Extra comfort, less hassle. Think pre-set tents, wooden cabins, with cozy beds, electricity, and a bit more space.
RV Camping
Prefer to bring your own home on wheels? Just park, plug in, and let the festival magic take over!

Electric Castle offers premium VIP and luxury ticket options for attendees who want an elevated festival experience. The VIP experience at Electric Castle is not just a ticket — it's taking in all the festival vibes in style, skipping lines not shows, and being where the spotlight is.

Three VIP pass tiers are available at Electric Castle: VIP Pass, Ultra VIP Pass, and Black Ticket.

## VIP LOUNGE

Electric Castle VIP Lounge — the core of the VIP experience at the Castle. Here you get the best of both worlds: epic performances and an exclusive place to be part of them. Where you can let go just as you would in the middle of the crowd but with a premium twist.
VIP Lounge Access at Electric Castle: Thursday to Sunday, 5 PM to 4 AM.

## EPIC VIEW TO MAINSTAGE

Electric Castle VIP perk — epic view to the Main Stage. VIP ticket holders get access to a premium viewing area with the best sightlines to the Main Stage, so you don't miss a second of your favorite band's performance.

## COCKTAIL BAR

Electric Castle VIP Cocktail Bar — exclusive bar for VIP ticket holders. Try something fancy and drink it with your pinky up.

## NAUGHTY-WEATHER-FREE LOUNGE

Electric Castle VIP covered lounge — protection from rain and weather. VIP ticket holders enjoy a sheltered area with zero chances of getting wet. It's always sunny in the lounge.

## PREMIUM TOILETS WITH VANITIES

Electric Castle VIP premium toilets — dedicated clean facilities with vanity areas for VIP ticket holders. Go in style. Just like at home.

## CHARGING SPOTS

Electric Castle VIP phone charging stations — dedicated charging spots for VIP ticket holders. All phone bars happy.

## FOOD À LA CARTE

Electric Castle VIP food service — à la carte dining available exclusively for VIP ticket holders. Not your mom's food. But she will most certainly approve of our Chef's cooking.

## SELECTED CRAFT BEERS & WINE & SPIRITS

Electric Castle VIP drinks selection — specialty craft beers, wine, and spirits available for VIP ticket holders. Plenty of specialty drinks. Deciding will be tough.

## SPECIALTY COFFEE SPOT

Electric Castle VIP specialty coffee — a dedicated coffee spot for VIP ticket holders. Because there are coffees, and coffees.

## EXCLUSIVE AFTER PARTY

Electric Castle VIP exclusive after party — when the Main Stage drops the drapes, the beat goes on for VIP ticket holders. High heels not recommended.

## VIP PASS

Electric Castle VIP Pass — the entry-level premium tier at Electric Castle. Gives you access to the premium lounge with some extra sauce. Includes all VIP Lounge amenities above plus:

Separate entrance — skip the lines at Electric Castle, not the shows.
Dedicated parking lots — your car where it deserves.
Vanity area near the Castle — just in case.

## ULTRA VIP PASS

Electric Castle Ultra VIP Pass — the mid-tier premium ticket at Electric Castle. The VIP pass topped with awesomeness. Includes all VIP Pass perks plus:

Private shuttle service — dedicated shuttle for all Ultra VIP holders at certain hours. No need to take the EC bus to the festival.
Free VIP parking spot — for Very Important Cars at Electric Castle.
Special Ultra VIP wristband.
Private concierge for festival tours — a concierge will get in touch a few days before the festival with all the details.

## BLACK TICKET

Electric Castle Black Ticket — the ultimate luxury experience at Electric Castle. All the VIP and Ultra VIP perks combined with confetti on top. The most exclusive way to experience Electric Castle festival.

5 Stars Hotel — sleep like a rockstar. Hotel accommodation is included for Black Ticket holders.
Private personal shuttle service — no need to designate a driver. Door-to-door transport to Electric Castle.
Free VIP parking spot at Electric Castle.
Food & Drink top-up — just keep them coming. Pre-loaded wristband credit for food and drinks at the festival.
Free access to all festival experiences — roam around freely at Electric Castle.
EC merchandise — wear it proudly.
Dedicated Angel — whatever you need, whenever you need it during the festival.
Meet & greet the EC team — hang out with the Electric Castle organizers.
A concierge will get in touch a few days before the festival with all the details.

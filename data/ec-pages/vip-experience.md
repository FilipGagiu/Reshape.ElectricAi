The VIP experience at Electric Castle is not just a ticket — it's taking in all the festival vibes in style, skipping lines not shows, and being where the spotlight is.

Three pass tiers are available: VIP Pass, Ultra VIP Pass, and Black Ticket.

## VIP LOUNGE

The core of the VIP experience at the Castle. Here you get the best of both worlds: epic performances and an exclusive place to be part of them. Where you can let go just as you would in the middle of the crowd but with a premium twist.
VIP Lounge Access: Thursday to Sunday, 5 PM to 4 AM.

## VIP PASS

Gives you access to the premium lounge with some extra sauce. Includes all VIP Lounge amenities above plus:

Separate entrance — skip the lines, not the shows.
Dedicated parking lots — your car where it deserves.
Vanity area near the Castle — just in case.

## ULTRA VIP PASS

The VIP pass topped with awesomeness. Includes all VIP Pass perks plus:

Private shuttle service — dedicated shuttle for all Ultra VIP holders at certain hours. No need to take the EC bus.
Free VIP parking spot — for Very Important Cars.
Special Ultra VIP wristband.
Private concierge for festival tours — a concierge will get in touch a few days before the festival with all the details.

## BLACK TICKET

The ultimate experience. All the VIP and Ultra VIP perks combined with confetti on top.

5 Stars Hotel — sleep like a rockstar.
Private personal shuttle service — no need to designate a driver.
Free VIP parking spot.
Food & Drink top-up — just keep them coming.
Free access to all festival experiences — roam around freely.
EC merchandise — wear it proudly.
Dedicated Angel — whatever you need, whenever you need it.
Meet & greet the EC team — hang out with us.
A concierge will get in touch a few days before the festival with all the details.

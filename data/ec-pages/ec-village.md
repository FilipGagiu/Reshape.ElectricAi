EC Village is the official on-site campsite of Electric Castle festival, located directly at the Banffy Castle festival grounds in Bonțida, Romania. Staying in EC Village means no daily commute between Cluj-Napoca and Bonțida, no last bus to catch, no group to gather — one more song is always possible. EC Village opens on Wednesday, the day before the festival starts.

## Camping

EC Village Camping — all friends in one place. Camping at Electric Castle isn't just a crash spot, it's sunrise-to-sunrise bonding, living that full festival glow-up. Pitch your own tent or skip the setup hassle with our pre-pitched ones.
Festival Pass + Camping Pass are mandatory for each person staying in the EC Village camping area.

## Car Camping

EC Village Car Camping — less commuting, more fun at Electric Castle. Imagine a little parcel of 3x8 meters to park your car, pitch a tent and set up anything else you might need to feel cozy. Ideal for festival-goers who want to bring extra gear and stay comfortable.
Car Camping ticket + Festival Pass + Camping Pass are mandatory for each person in the Car Camping area.

## RV Camping

EC Village RV Camping — your base camp round the corner at Electric Castle. Bring your home on wheels at the festival and park it in a designated area of the EC Village. You will have a power source, a water source nearby (you'll need your own hose), wastewater discharge, and a chemical toilet discharge point.
RV Camping ticket + Festival Pass + Camping Pass are mandatory for each participant in the RV area.

## Glamping

EC Village Glamping — city comfort at Electric Castle. All the comfort of a hotel room — luxury lays in making the best out of every minute. Enjoy freedom and comfort in a quieter area of EC Village, with pre-set tents or wooden cabins that include beds, electricity, and more space than regular camping.
Valid accommodation ticket, a Festival Pass + Camping Pass are mandatory for each person sleeping in all the accommodation structures.

## What to pack

Recommended packing list for Electric Castle EC Village camping:

Festival Pass and Camping Pass — keep them close.
Tent — if you stay in the tent area.
Sleeping bag — it might get cold during the nights.
Sturdy boots — useful for mud, dancing and long walks.
A warm jacket and a blanket.
Sun protection.
Waterproof coat — weather sometimes has its own line-up.
Toiletries — for the EC Village showers.
Enough clothes — especially if your plan is "just one more night".
Money/cards — top-ups on your wristband; do not expect to use cash inside the festival area and EC Village.
Medical info — if you have any allergies or medical conditions.
Medication if required — do not leave it at home.
Original ID documents — needed for swapping your ticket with the wristband(s).
Bags for dirty clothes and rubbish — do not leave dirty clothes unattended, our cleaning staff might confuse them with garbage.

## What's not allowed to pack

Items banned from EC Village campsite at Electric Castle:

Drugs (illegal substances)
Fire sources
Electric generators — staff will assist if needed
Outdoor cooking appliances
Electric cooling systems
Umbrellas of any kind
Covered pavilions
Large fans or electric air conditioning devices
Weapons, explosives (even if legally detained)
Knives, penknives
Glass containers (except perfumes)
Alcohol
Pets or animals
Bicycles, skates, scooters, hoverboards, segways
Musical instruments
Garden furniture (except small folding chairs and tables)
Big sound systems
Reusable metal or glass bottles
Drones or other unmanned aerial vehicles

## Our tips

Tips for a great Electric Castle EC Village experience:

Traveling light: you will have to carry your stuff from the parking area or the bus station to EC Village and back — that's 10 to 20 minutes walk. Bring a mountain backpack rather than a trolley; the EC Village is not an asphalted area.
Use luggage tags — it will be easier to track your belongings in the EC Village area and surroundings.
Be patient — for security reasons, bags may be searched. Please cooperate and keep luggage unwrapped and accessible.
Bring a sturdy tent if you're camping.
Reuse. Reduce. Recycle. — nature is our dance floor. Avoid single-use items and opt for reusables instead.
Look for volunteers and the info point whenever you have questions or need help inside EC Village.

## How to make it to EC Village

EC Village has a separate entrance from the main Electric Castle festival entrance. Whether you're coming by bus or car, check the map on the EC website to find your way around. The EC Village entrance is separate from the main festival entrance.

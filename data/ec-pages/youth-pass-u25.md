The Youth Pass U25 gives general access for the full festival period at a special price for attendees aged 25 or under on the last day of the festival.

Some moments stay with us forever. First kiss, first time camping or attending the concert of your favorite artists. We value the impact of these experiences and want to help all the youngsters create the best memories at Electric Castle's edition by offering them a pass at a special price.

Price: €139 + tax (early bird) / €200 + tax (standard)

You are eligible for this ticket if you'll be (maximum) 25 years old on the last day of the festival.
• The information you provide when buying the ticket must correspond to your ID info.
• At the swapping point, in order to receive your festival wristband, you will have to confirm your name and birth date by showing your ID.

Children up to 7 years old (inclusive) have free access at Electric Castle, as long as they are accompanied by a parent/guardian with a valid festival pass.

## Rules

The Youth Pass U25 is nominal and it offers general access for the whole festival period.
The name, street, city and birth date filled in when buying the ticket must correspond with the ones from your ID.
The Youth Pass U25 ticket can be transferred on the exchange only to individuals who meet the age requirement.
Make sure that you add your information correctly. You can only change the information on your ticket in case of a typo, by contacting us at contact@electriccastle.ro

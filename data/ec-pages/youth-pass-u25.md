Electric Castle Youth Pass U25 is a discounted festival ticket available exclusively for young attendees aged 25 or under. The Youth Pass U25 gives general access to Electric Castle for the full festival period at a special price for attendees aged 25 or under on the last day of the festival.

Some moments stay with us forever. First kiss, first time camping or attending the concert of your favorite artists. We value the impact of these experiences and want to help all the youngsters create the best memories at Electric Castle's edition by offering them a pass at a special price.

Electric Castle Youth Pass U25 price: €139 + tax (early bird) / €200 + tax (standard)

Eligibility for the Electric Castle Youth Pass U25: You are eligible for this ticket if you'll be (maximum) 25 years old on the last day of the festival.
• The information you provide when buying the Youth Pass U25 ticket must correspond to your ID info.
• At the swapping point, in order to receive your festival wristband, you will have to confirm your name and birth date by showing your ID.

Children up to 7 years old (inclusive) have free access at Electric Castle, as long as they are accompanied by a parent/guardian with a valid festival pass.

## Rules

Rules and conditions for the Electric Castle Youth Pass U25 ticket:

The Youth Pass U25 is nominal and it offers general access to Electric Castle for the whole festival period.
The name, street, city and birth date filled in when buying the Youth Pass U25 ticket must correspond with the ones from your ID.
The Youth Pass U25 ticket can be transferred on the exchange only to individuals who meet the age requirement (25 or under on the last day of the festival).
Make sure that you add your information correctly. You can only change the information on your Youth Pass U25 ticket in case of a typo, by contacting us at contact@electriccastle.ro

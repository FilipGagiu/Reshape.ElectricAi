Nature Is Our Dance Floor. That's why, when the party is over, we pay attention to everything we leave behind, so future generations of festival goers will be able to rock their bodies in the exact pristine nature and, most importantly, live on a rockin' planet.

At Electric Castle, we believe that when it comes to any sustainable action it shouldn't be a one-hit wonder, but an all-timer. Our environmental efforts are constant and more in tune with nature — one edition at a time.

Our take on sustainability is community-based. We engage in different initiatives and partnerships with NGOs, associations, and key partners all year long to bring about real change.

## CARPOOLING

CO2 slayer on board — 6,000 cars at full capacity. Jump in a friend's or neighbour's car and drive together to the Castle during the 4 days of the festival to reduce carbon emissions. Last year 6,000 cars arrived at the festival packed with friends and won free parking, and some even won EC12 tickets, VIP upgrades, or official merchandise.

## COLLECTIVE TRANSPORT

EC train: 18,872 people came by train. Leave the car at home and hop on the EC train — the party will start on your way to the Castle while helping reduce CO2 emissions.
EC buses: 30,000 festival goers took the bus. Free yourself from the hustle of driving and hop on our non-stop buses.

## GREENERY

Evergrowing Forest: Over 7,000 trees planted. Together with BT, we started to transform EC Village into a place with many shaded areas and green spaces. This year we planted 2,500 trees, with a total of 7,000 trees planted since 2023.
The Green Carpet: 12 ha replanted with grass seed. Grass protects against soil erosion, absorbs water, and helps purify the air. Together with OMV, we replant the festival's site with 12 ha of fresh grass each year.
Together with Lidl, our strategic partner, we are committed to a more environmentally responsible festival experience — making every beat, every light, and every moment more sustainable.

## LESS PLASTIC

Life in plastic AIN'T fantastic. We aim to reduce single-use plastic in the festival and put into motion all actions to minimise plastic usage whenever possible. All providers, food vendors, and vendors follow the Less Plastic Policy.
Water Refill Station: 2 tons of plastic cut down in 2024. Inside the EC village you'll find a water refill station you can use at any time to refill your camelback or water bottle.

## SELECTIVE WASTE

+117 tons of garbage sorted out. Look for the colorful bins! Lidl helps people keep the festival grounds squeaky clean by covering the area with recycling bins. 20% of waste was recycled.

## WASTE MANAGEMENT

"Clean up after ourselves" pledge. Building the festival from the ground up uses a lot of resources — we aim to recycle the pre & post festival waste.
Cooking oil turned into candles: with the help of OilRight, we collected nearly a ton (965 kg) of used cooking oil from our vendors, which is now being turned into eco-friendly candles.

## UPCYCLING

Electric Castle's sustainable merch line is made out of recycled and reused materials from the festival.
Rematerialization: construction-used materials get a second life. What today was flooring, tomorrow can be a bar counter.
A fashion upcycling campaign: worn-out clothes get the second life they deserve — donated garments become new outfits, tote bags and bean bags.

## ECO EC VILLAGE

Timed Hot Showers: 2.5 million liters of water saved. Our timed hot showers save 30% of water and energy.
Sustainable lighting: camping alleys are illuminated with solar-powered lamps that last all night.
Solar totems: photovoltaic charging stations spread around the campsite — just plug in and let nature power your phone.
Ashtray Polls: over 50,000 cigarette butts were collected by the fun yellow polls in the festival in 2024.

## COMMUNITY

Creating sustainable communities isn't just about the now; it's about laying the foundation of a place where future generations can live too. Local communities play a vital role in driving sustainable development initiatives, in and outside the festival, as they are intimately connected to the natural resources, social fabric, and economic well-being of their regions.

## VOLUNTEERING

Our volunteers? Absolute heroes. They're the go-to for everything festival-related, from lockers to wristband top-ups, pouring their hearts into making EC unforgettable. Annually, 1,000 strong, they're the backbone of our festival and the future of the event industry.

## RISING STARS

With almost 35% of the line-up consisting of local & emerging artists, we're all about shining a spotlight on emerging stars, giving up-and-coming artists a platform to wow the world, a shot to move people.

## KIDS CORNER

EC is a playground for adults, but kids have their own corner of fun too. By partnering with different associations every year, we've designed a space to stimulate creativity and joy and keep the little EC fans occupied, and their parents in the festival vibe.

## ALIAT CHILL TENT

Your ally for a healthy sustainable living, free of alcohol and other addictions (Alianța pentru Lupta Împotriva Alcoolismului și Toxicomaniilor).

## CASTLE MARKET

The Castle Market is our shout-out to sustainability — a cozy spot for local crafts and brands that stick with sustainability principles. With upcycled fashion, handmade jewelry, artisanal work and more, it's a place where small businesses get together to move EC fans towards conscious consumption.

## EC TALKS

A space where stories that change lives unfold — hosting remarkable encounters with guests whose insights and experiences inspire and challenge us.

## CREATIVE CAMP

Born in August 2020, Creative Camp is our musical incubator. 3 weeks where new music blooms through collaboration between young blood and seasoned pros — proving that creativity knows no bounds.

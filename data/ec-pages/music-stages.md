Electric Castle festival features multiple music stages spread across the Banffy Castle grounds in Bonțida, Romania. Each stage has its own identity, programming style, and atmosphere. The stages run 24/7 during the festival, with overlapping schedules across different genres.

## MAIN STAGE

Electric Castle Main Stage — the largest and most iconic stage at Electric Castle, sponsored by Coca-Cola. Our biggest stage is where the most awaited artists perform. Here you can enjoy the most spectacular EC moments, sharing climax with thousands of people like you. Thrilling.
At the Electric Castle Main Stage you can listen to: the most spectacular live shows and DJ sets spanning mainstream electronic, rock, hip-hop, and pop.
Artists who played at the Main Stage: MASSIVE ATTACK, KHRUANGBIN, CHASE AND STATUS, TWENTY ONE PILOTS, GORILLAZ, MACKLEMORE, IGGY POP, THE PRODIGY, SIGUR ROS, SKRILLEX, DEADMAU5, CARIBOU, ALT-J, DAMIAN MARLEY, BRING ME THE HORIZON, LIMP BIZKIT, THIRTY SECONDS TO MARS, FLORENCE + THE MACHINE, FRANZ FERDINAND, PAUL KALKBRENNER.

## HANGAR

Electric Castle Hangar Stage — an indoor stage sponsored by Banca Transilvania. Welcoming a mix of punks, ravers, artists, and music lovers from all walks of life. It's not just about partying—it's about connection, creativity, and shared energy.
At the Electric Castle Hangar Stage you can listen to: alternative & niche live acts and sophisticated DJ sets including rock, alternative electronic, and experimental music.
Artists who played at the Hangar Stage: MARC REBILLET, DJ SHADOW, SHAQ AKA DJ DIESEL, ORBITAL, ALTIN GUN, MOGWAI, SQUID, Genesis Owusu, Cavalera, Apashe & Brass Orchestra, WHOMADEWHO, KID FRANCESCOLI, PALAYE ROYALE, L'Entourloop, IDLES, YUNG LEAN, THE HU, NOGA EREZ, GUS GUS, VIAGRA BOYS.

## BOOHA

Electric Castle Booha Stage — the iconic industrial-look stage sponsored by glo, and the first thing you see when entering the festival. This is where the party never stops at Electric Castle. The programming spans various sub-genres, ensuring a rich and eclectic musical experience.
At the Electric Castle Booha Stage you can listen to: house, groovy house, bass, disco and melodic techno.
Artists who played at the Booha Stage: Bonobo DJ Set, CARLITA, Francis Mercier, TODD TERJE, BARRY CAN'T SWIM, HAAI, BOOKA SHADE, EWAN MCVICAR, BEN BOHMER, MIND AGAINST, DAVID PENN, SAM DIVINE, LENZMAN, NASTIA, CHRIS LORENZO, MIKE SKINNER, MY NU LENG, CRAZY P, ELI ESCOBAR, MJ COLE.

## HIDEOUT

Electric Castle Hideout Stage — an intimate outdoor stage sponsored by #UNLOCKWONDER. The crowd at the Hideout is typically a mix of jet-setters, bohemian travelers, spiritual seekers, and influencers — drawn by EC's unique blend of natural beauty, music, and the opportunity to experience events that feel both exclusive and authentic.
At the Electric Castle Hideout Stage you can listen to: afro house, organic house & deep tribal house.
Artists who played at the Hideout Stage: Sparrow and Barbossa, Caiiro, Moblack, Elif, Awen, Nasiri, JAMIIE.

## THE BEACH

Electric Castle The Beach Stage — an underground and intimate stage for avant-garde and niche music at Electric Castle. Focused on underground and avant-garde artists rather than mainstream headliners, maintaining an authentic appeal. Creates a more intimate atmosphere, ensuring high-quality sound and an engaged crowd. Lineups are carefully crafted to balance established legends with up-and-coming talents.
At the Electric Castle Beach Stage you can listen to: caribbean sounds, funk, bossa breaks, trip hop, rare grooves, disco & boogie, northern soul & funk, soulful garage & liquid drum and bass, oriental sounds.
Artists who played at The Beach Stage: CALIBRE, DJ KRUSH, MARK FARINA, DANILO PLESSOW (MCDE), GILLES PETERSON, GREG WILSON, NICOLA CONTE, QUANTIC, RENEGADES OF JAZZ, BOCA 45, AUTARKIC, KHIDJA, FANTASTIC TWINS LIVE, KATZELE & ASAF SAMUEL, QUE SAKAMOTO, PALMS TRAX, KRUDER & DORFMEISTER, NIGHTMARES ON WAX, NATASHA DIGGS, DJ MARKY.

## BACKYARD STAGE

Electric Castle Backyard Stage — a soulful outdoor stage sponsored by MobilaDalin, nestled in a garden-like setting with fairy lights and lush greenery. The Backyard Stage is a haven where authentic, inspiring sounds come alive. Each act takes you on a journey, from pulsing basslines to delicate strings and unforgettable vocals, all raw and organic.
At the Electric Castle Backyard Stage you can listen to: African rhythms, Latin grooves, Asian melodies, and European avant-garde.
Artists who played at the Backyard Stage: KAWALA, iLe, FULU MIZIKI, OSCAR JEROME, VALERIA STOICA, LOS BITCHOS, GAYE SU AKYOL, QUERALT LAHOZ, MDOU MOCTAR, NICOLA CONTE & BAND, ANTONY SZMIEREK, ANGELICA GARCIA, NIGHT BEATS, JOYCUT, SABABA 5.

## PING PONG STAGE

Electric Castle Ping Pong Stage — a fun, nostalgic stage sponsored by Burn. The oldies but goldies hits we all love to hear once in a while are at Electric Castle's Ping Pong Stage. Here you'll involuntarily shake your body right from the first beat.
At the Electric Castle Ping Pong Stage you can listen to: everyone's guilty pleasures — pop classics, party hits, and fun crowd-pleasing sets.
Artists who played at the Ping Pong Stage: KRAFTY KUTS, HUEY MORGAN, DJ ANTENNA, ANDI MOISESCU, DJ HEFE, DJ MA-TA, MeloMelutii, Dj Phantom, DISCOTECĂ FT., DANA MARIJUANA, OTRAVĂ.

## CAMPING STAGE

Electric Castle Camping Stage — a daytime stage located inside EC Village, the festival's campsite. Get the sunshine and the good vibes. Match your morning coffee with the right music, starting from 12:00 PM. A more relaxed, intimate stage experience within the campsite itself.
At the Electric Castle Camping Stage you can listen to: fresh tunes to wake up your body and soul and surprise acoustic performances.
Artists who played at the Camping Stage: Om la Lună (acoustic), Fără Zahăr, Vița de Vie (acoustic), DAN MEDEȘAN, EZIRK, ELLEN, ELIAN, BRUSTEN, SOUR, SHU.
